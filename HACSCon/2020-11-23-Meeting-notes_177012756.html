Research Question

  • Based on the comments made by the professor and TA last class, and taking a look at the data we have gathered, we decided to change the research question

  • Because we have gathered a significant amount of bot attacks, we want to look at the complexity of the attack (# of commands, duration of attack) and compare it to the time of the attack to see if there is a time of day with the most high risk attacks

  • We also want to see if the location of the attacks make a difference by looking up the IP addresses

  • Furthermore, we discussed that because we have a lot of recurring bot attacks, we can compare the data between them

  • For the same bot, we can look at the time they are attacking, the complexity of their code and malware dropped, the number of attacks we have received from them, and the number of unique IP addresses they have
